About Us

INTERTROPICS Security Services & Investigations is a trusted leader in providing professional security solutions. Our comprehensive services include surveillance, disaster preparedness, private investigations, and more. We strive to ensure your safety and peace of mind with top-notch expertise and state-of-the-art technology.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and professional security solutions that safeguard lives and property, ensuring peace of mind for all our clients.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and reputable security services provider, setting the highest standards of safety and security in the region.

Our Services

EyeWatch Surveillance & CCTV Installation

Ensure complete oversight and protection of your premises with EyeWatch Surveillance & CCTV Installation. At INTERTROPICS, we specialize in the professional installation and monitoring of high-definition CCTV cameras that provide 24/7 surveillance.

Mobile Patrol Services

Our mobile patrol services offer swift and flexible security solutions, ensuring constant surveillance across large areas. With a team of trained officers and rapid response vehicles, INTERTROPICS provides proactive protection, deterring threats before they happen.

Event Security Management

Ensure the safety and success of your event with INTERTROPICS' expert event security management. Our professional team is trained to handle crowd control, access management, and emergency response, providing seamless protection for events of all sizes.

Disaster Response & Preparedness

INTERTROPICS' disaster response and preparedness services equip your organization to handle emergencies with confidence. Our expert team provides rapid response, risk assessments, and strategic planning to minimize damage and protect lives during crises.

Professional Security Guards

Our team of Professional Security Guards is trained to deliver high-quality protection services. They are equipped with the skills needed to handle various security situations, including patrolling, monitoring, and responding to incidents swiftly and efficiently.

Training Programs

Fire Marshal Training

Our Fire Marshal Training equips participants with essential skills to handle fire emergencies. From risk assessments to using fire-fighting equipment, our comprehensive program ensures you are prepared to safeguard lives and property in case of a fire.

Security Guard Training

We offer professional Security Guard Training to ensure our guards are equipped with the latest techniques in conflict resolution, crowd management, and emergency response. Our training guarantees effective and efficient security personnel for any situation.
